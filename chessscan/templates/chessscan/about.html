About

Here's how it works:

  • Take a screenshot of your current chessboard position.
  • Choose between two different chess piece formats to match your preference and the style of your chessboard. Upload the image.
  • Using OpenCV template matching techniques, we accurately detect and analyze the position of each chess piece on the board.
  • You'll receive the FEN notation of the position.
  • You can continue your game on Lichess to further analyze the position.

* Accuracy might not be 100%
